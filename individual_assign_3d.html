3D Individual Assignment

I have chosen to design a mini salt and pepper shaker keychain that is about 30mm tall and 12mm wide. I took inspiration from an hourglass to be able to design the salt and pepper shaker as I wanted it to be abit different than a normal salt and pepper shaker. The lids of the shaker can be removed and theres a small hole inside to make it seem like an actual shaker.

Here is how i designed my salt and pepper shaker

  1. I first created a sketch of a quarter part of the shaker
  2. Using the revolve tool, I then made the half hour glass shape
  3. Using spilt body tool, I then spilt the body so that I would come up with a lid and the shaker body
  4. Using Circle tool & Circular pattern, I then made holes in the lid to look like a normal shaker lid
  5. Then I made the inserts to make the lid detachable from the shaker body using the circle, offset and extrude tool
  6. I then mirror the half of the shaker body and lid so that it would have a top half, and now both shaker bodies are joined together while the lids remain separate bodies
  7. I then sketch the handle and extrude it symmetrically on both sides
  8. Lastly, I added the Text P and S to indicate which side is the salt and pepper
  9. And there is my mini salt and pepper shaker keychain!
