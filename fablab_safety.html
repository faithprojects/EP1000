FABLAB Safety

Why is it important?

It is important to be aware of the risks and hazards and to be safe when operating equipment in the fablabs. Without knowing this, you wont be able to use the fablab equipment and facilities.

Safety Measures that I learnt

Proper Attire for Both males and females

  • Long hair must be tied up
  • No loose items allowed (e.g. hanging necklace)
  • T-shirt (no long sleeves, if so pull/fold up sleeves)
  • Hands free of adornments (e.g. loose bracelet)
  • Long pants
  • Covered shoes (anti slip soles if possible)

PPE available for loan in FabLab

  • Safety apron
  • Disposable gloves
  • Heat resistant gloves
  • Safety glasses
  • Rubber re-usable gloves
  • Anti-cut gloves

Safety Warning Placards

LOW Risk

  • User must be trained in the use of the machine
  • No supervision required

MEDIUM Risk

  • User must be trained in the use of the machine
  • Moderate severity of potential hazard

HIGH Risk

  • User must be trained in the use of the machine
  • Supervision Required
  • High/Catastrophic Severity of the hazard

Emergency procedures

  • If alarm sounds while working, IMMEDIATELY STOP WORK & wait for instructions from Staff-in-charge
  • If an uncontrollable fire starts in Fab Lab, IMMEDIATELY INFORM everyone in the lab to leave
  • In the event of emergency & incident reporting, seek assistance of the Staff in-charge of Fab Lab
  • If staff is not around, call
    • SP Emergency Hotline 6772 1234
    • Ambulance & SCDF 995
    • Police 999

Housekeeping practices

  • Always return the tools back to their designated position in the tool racks after use
  • Always keep the workplace neat & orderly after use
  • Always clear away the debris and chips off the machines or workbenches after use
  • Always bring unused or leftover materials to the recycle area

Grade of quiz

What is the score required?

We need to get a score of 80% and higher to be able to pass the Safety Quiz and get our safety skill updated in FabLab Management System to then not only use the facilities in the FabLabs but also be able to register for the other skill courses like 3D printer or laser cutting. I took about 3 attempts to do the quiz and on the third attempt I passed the test with a score of 85% woohoo!
